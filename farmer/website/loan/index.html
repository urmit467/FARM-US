Dear farmers , many of you are facing serious debt problems because of several challenges. First, loans from banks and private moneylenders are often taken for seeds, fertilizers, or equipment, but when crops fail due to bad weather or diseases, the income is not enough to repay them. Private lenders also charge high interest rates, which makes the debt grow quickly. Even when income increases, like in some parts of Punjab, many farmers remain in debt because of rising costs and poor market prices. In some states like Karnataka, this has sadly led to farmer suicides. It is important to understand these issues so we can find better solutions and support systems together.
